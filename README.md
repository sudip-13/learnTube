# LearnTube

## Description

LearnTube is a user-friendly platform offering engaging videos, personalized learning paths, save-for-later feature, and free courses to enhance your knowledge journey.

## Features

- **Watch Videos**: Users can watch a variety of educational videos.
- **Liked Videos**: Users can like videos and view them later in the liked videos section.
- **Save for Later**: Users can save videos to watch later.
- **Free Courses**: Users have access to a variety of free courses.
- - **Paid Courses**:Users can also access to a Premium Course after payment.
 - **Purchase Courses**: Users can purchase premium courses securely through the Stripe payment gateway.
